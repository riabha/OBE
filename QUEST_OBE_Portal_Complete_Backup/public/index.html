What is Outcome-Based Education?

Outcome-Based Education (OBE) is an educational approach that focuses on what students should know and be able to do by the end of their educational experience.

Clear Learning Outcomes

Define specific, measurable learning outcomes that students must achieve to graduate successfully.

Continuous Assessment

Regular evaluation and feedback to ensure students are meeting the defined learning outcomes.

Continuous Improvement

Regular review and enhancement of curriculum based on assessment results and feedback.
